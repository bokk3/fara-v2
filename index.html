My Services

Sport Coaching

Personalized sport coaching designed to help you reach your athletic goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, I provide tailored training programs that focus on technique, strength, and performance improvement.

  • One-on-one personalized training sessions
  • Customized fitness and nutrition plans
  • Performance tracking and goal setting
  • Technique refinement and skill development
  • Injury prevention and recovery guidance

Dance Teaching

Express yourself through movement with professional dance instruction. From beginners to advanced dancers, I offer classes in various styles that combine technical skill with creative expression and performance confidence.

  • Multiple dance styles (ballet, contemporary, jazz)
  • Individual and group lessons available
  • Choreography for performances and events
  • Flexibility and body conditioning training
  • Performance preparation and stage presence

About Me

About Fara Truyens

With over 10 years of experience in both sport coaching and dance instruction, I am passionate about helping individuals discover their potential and achieve their fitness and artistic goals. My journey began as a competitive athlete and dancer, which gave me firsthand insight into the dedication and guidance needed to excel in these fields.

I believe in a holistic approach that combines technical skill development with mental strength and creative expression. Whether you're training for competition, improving your fitness, or exploring dance as a form of self-expression, I'm here to support and guide you every step of the way.

Qualifications & Certifications

  • Certified Personal Trainer (CPT) - National Academy of Sports Medicine
  • Level 3 Sports Coach - UK Coaching
  • Professional Dance Teaching Diploma - Royal Academy of Dance
  • Certified Nutrition Coach - Precision Nutrition
  • First Aid & CPR Certified

Experience

Throughout my career, I have worked with clients of all ages and skill levels, from beginners taking their first steps to advanced athletes and dancers preparing for competitions. I have coached youth sports teams, choreographed performances for dance companies, and helped countless individuals transform their health and confidence through personalized training programs at MoveToFit.be.
